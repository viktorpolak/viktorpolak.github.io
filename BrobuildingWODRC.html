Ronnie's Workout and Diet plan

To get big you have to eat big. This is exactly what Ronnie Coleman did. He ate over 5500 calories a day, 150g of fats, 546g of protein and 474g of carbs. Big Ron got this nickname for a reason. It takes a lot of dicipline to be able to get this intake of calories every day, a very few selection of people are able to do it and even less people can do it only eating lean food.

Ronnie's get big workout has 2 rest days. This is probably because of the intesity of his workout plan, which features a lot of exercises for each gym session. As you may know rest and sleep is one of the pillars of getting big and that is why 2 rest days are essential.
